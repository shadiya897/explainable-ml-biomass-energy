# Explainable AI & ML for Biomass Energy Assessment

## Overview
Built and compared 9 ML models on 36 years of climate and coconut production data to predict biomass energy potential with explainable AI.

## Tools Used
Python, Scikit-learn, SHAP, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Results
- Best model R² = 0.8663, reducing prediction error by 53%
- Applied SHAP across 1,000+ simulations
- Estimated 72,000 MWh/°C energy impact
- Potential reduction of 2.46M tonnes/year CO₂ emissions
